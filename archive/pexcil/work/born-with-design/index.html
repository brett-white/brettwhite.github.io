Born With Design

Flash Site, Mobile Site & Brand

  • Info

    Born With Design was the original brand identity of PEXCIL, from 2007 to 2013. The primary focus having been Flash based, interactive websites. With the growth of HTML5, and the subsequent loss of interest in Flash, it was time for us to evolve too. The Flash and mobile site won numerous awards.

  • Role

    Concept, Design, Development

  • Tech

    Flash AS2, XML, PHP, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery Mobile

  • Tools

    Photoshop, Illustrator, Flash, Dreamweaver, Soundbooth
