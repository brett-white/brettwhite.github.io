About

I trained as a graphic designer. Then I taught myself to code. Then I ran a fintech company. These aren't separate chapters — they're the same instinct applied at different scales. At every stage I've been trying to understand the full picture: what something should look like, how it actually gets built, and whether it makes sense as a business. That combination turned out to be useful in ways I didn't plan for.

Most of my career played out at Zapper, a South African payments platform where I joined as a developer and left as CEO — with stints running design, product, and commercial in between. By the time I became CEO I'd touched every layer of the business, which made the hard decisions a little less mysterious. During those three years: we migrated the platform from hardware to cloud (cutting operating costs by 82%), grew the user base from 1.5M to 13M, expanded the merchant network from 30K to 150K, launched South Africa's first independent Tap-on-Phone solution, and took the App Store rating from 3.4 to 4.8. Revenue grew 30% in the first 12 months. I also hold two fintech patents from this period.

At Clickatell I led payments, AI, and product innovation globally — building the Chat Commerce AI engine, designing a cross-regional payments orchestration strategy, and restructuring the Nigerian commercial model in a way that delivered 10x gross profit growth on the same transaction volume.

I spent three years on South Africa's QR Code Standardisation Committee. Turns out that's how payment infrastructure actually gets made: slow, contested, occasionally frustrating, and worth it.

I'm currently Head of Regulated Canada for Super Group (NYSE: SGHC), leading Ontario and Alberta across Betway, Jackpot City, and a portfolio of casino brands.

National Diploma in Graphic Design, Durban University of Technology — the foundation of everything that followed.

  • Chief Executive Officer

    Zapper - Cape Town, South Africa | Aug 2019 - Aug 2022

    I joined Zapper as a developer. I left as CEO. In between I ran design, product, and commercial — which meant that when I finally ran the company, I'd already broken most of the things that could go wrong.

    Led a full platform migration from a hardware-dependent solution to a cloud-hosted API architecture, reducing operating costs by 82% and significantly improving system reliability. Grew the merchant network 5x from 30K to 150K and the consumer base 8x from 1.5M to 13M through platform partnerships with major banks and fintech apps — driving 30% revenue growth within the first 12 months.

    Pioneered South Africa's first independent Tap-on-Phone (SoftPOS) payment solution, securing regulatory approvals and establishing key industry partnerships. Redesigned the Zapper mobile app using customer research, improving the App Store rating from 3.4 to 4.8 and increasing engagement by 40%.
